## Content Format Standards

Every post MUST include:
- [ ] Keyword-rich H1 title
- [ ] Bolded direct-answer sentence within first 100 words (AEO)
- [ ] At least 1 comparison table
- [ ] FAQ section at the bottom (minimum 3 Q&A pairs)
- [ ] Internal links to 2+ related posts and 1 pillar page
- [ ] `pubDate` and `tags` in frontmatter
