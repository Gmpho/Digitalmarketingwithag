## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Google algorithm update penalizes thin content | Medium | Keep all posts 1,200+ words; update regularly |
| AI Overviews reduce click-through | High | Focus on AI citation as KPI; build email list |
| Social automation platforms restrict APIs | Medium | Don't depend on a single platform; use Zernio |
| Content copied by competitors | Low | Publish original data; establish author entity |
| Monetization denied by ad networks | Low | Ezoic has no traffic minimum; start there |
