*AEO targets are designed for direct extraction. Below are target queries, recommended formatting styles, and exact structural requirements.*

| # | Query | Recommended Answer Format | Target Output Guidelines |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | What is Generative Engine Optimization? | Definition | 1-2 sentence definition (bolded) in a separate paragraph (<60 words) |
| 2 | How does GEO differ from SEO? | Comparison Table | 3-column table: Attribute \| SEO \| GEO. |
| 3 | What are the top AI search engines in 2026? | Numbered List | Clean ordered HTML `<ol>` list: ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AIO, Claude, Gemini. |
| 4 | How do you optimize for ChatGPT Search? | Numbered Steps | 5-step process highlighting RSS feeds, webhooks, and citations. |
| 5 | What is the ideal paragraph length for AI extraction? | Direct Number | Clear statement: **134 to 167 words** with self-contained context. |
| 6 | How does Google AI Overviews cite sources? | Bulleted List | Unordered list of citation locations: carousel cards, inline links, side panels. |
| 7 | Does social media automation hurt brand reputation? | Yes/No + Context | Clear **No, when done right.** followed by 3 rules for human oversight. |
| 8 | What is Answer Engine Optimization? | Definition | 1-sentence definition starting with "**Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is...**" |
| 9 | How does Perplexity AI find sources? | Numbered Steps | 4-step diagrammatic explanation of Bing Index -> RAG -> Synthesizer. |
| 10 | What schema is required for voice search? | FAQPage / Speakable | Schema code block + explanation of `SpeakableSpecification`. |
| 11 | How do you build a content cluster for SEO? | Numbered Steps | Step 1-6 roadmap: Seed -> Clusters -> Pillar -> Internal Links -> Build. |
| 12 | What are E-E-A-T signals? | Bulleted List | Bullet points mapping Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness. |
| 13 | How can creators automate content repurposing? | Bulleted List | Workflow diagram of one long-form asset converting to 5 short ones. |
| 14 | What is the best automation tool for solopreneurs? | Comparison Table | Columns: Feature \| n8n \| Zapier \| Make. Focus on costs and limits. |
| 15 | How to monetize a newsletter in 2026? | Numbered Steps | 4 steps (Sponsorships -> Digital Products -> Subscriptions -> Ads). |
| 16 | Is affiliate marketing still profitable? | Yes/No + Stats | **Yes.** Citations of average ROI (18-22%) and programmatic changes. |

## 3. Cannibalization & Overlap Flags
- ⚠️ **"SEO vs GEO" Overlap:** Avoid writing multiple separate posts targeting "GEO benefits" and "SEO benefits". Consolidate into comparison tables inside the GEO pillar post.
- ⚠️ **"Social Schedulers" Overlap:** Do not write individual review articles for Hootsuite, Buffer, and Metricool. Instead, focus on a single core comparison guide ("Top AI Schedulers compared") and keep individual tool names as sub-headings to maintain page-level domain equity.
- ⚠️ **"Affiliate Marketing" Overlap:** The post on Digital Monetization already covers affiliate marketing. Do not create a separate "How to make money with affiliate marketing" post. Instead, build a specific cluster item targeting "Affiliate Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)" focusing on styling and click-through optimization.
